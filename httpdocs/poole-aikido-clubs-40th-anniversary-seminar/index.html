Seminars

Poole Aikido Club’s 40th anniversary seminar

Suzie Hibberd

·

17th September 2019

We’re very much looking forward to attending Poole Aikido Club’s 40th Anniversary Seminar at the end of November, and helping to celebrate Sensei Paul McGlone’s 50th year in Aikido! There will also be Dan tests on the Sunday, so it promises to be a big weekend!

It’s well worth booking ahead of time as there is a 25% early bird discount (pay before 8th Nov).

The prices for the Premier Inn, Holes Bay, Poole (where Sensei John will be staying) are also very reasonable at the moment – so good to get in quick!

Please let Sensei McGlone know ASAP if you would like to attend the celebration dinner on the Saturday evening – which will be held at the Grasshopper restaurant in Parkstone.

Please also note a slightly earlier lesson start time of 9:40am on the Saturday, and 9:30am on the Sunday. Make sure you arrive in good time! Registration is from 9:25am. Dan gradings will be at lunchtime on the Sunday.

We know weekend-long seminars can be tiring, but it’s well worth staying until the very end to support the organisation, and of course for all those extra nuggets of insight!

The booking form is available here.

Please see our Facebook page or talk to Sensei John for more information.

Hope to see you there!
